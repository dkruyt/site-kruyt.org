Instagram/Facebook HELO/SPF fail

I am trying to get an email from instagram, but I wasn’t receiving it. Upon checking my mail logs I see the following log message. Sep 13 03:50:51 mailserver postfix/smtpd[28105]: NOQUEUE: reject: RCPT from 66-220-155-156.mail-mail.facebook.com[66.220.155.156]: 550 5.7.1 <dennis@kruyt.org>: Recipient address rejected: Message rejected due to: domain owner discourages use of this host. Please see http://www.openspf.net/Why?s=helo;id=mx-out.facebook.com;ip=66.220.155.156;r=dennis@kruyt.org; from=<security@mail.instagram.com> to=<dennis@kruyt.org> proto=ESMTP helo=<mx-out.facebook.com> So the mail is SPF (Sender Policy Framework) failing.
